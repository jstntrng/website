Umbrella Kitchen

Homelessness in San Francisco is forever prominent. This restaurant identity is designed around giving those without a home, a place for shelter. The Umbrella Kitchen is a robin-hood restaurant that transforms its business into a soup kitchen on the weekends. The logo is a hermit crab character that finds a temporary home underneath an umbrella. The word mark is a drafted version of Bank Gothic, a typeface that is constructed to represent commerce while the patterns for the Umbrella Kitchen visually mimic the harsh San Francisco rain.
