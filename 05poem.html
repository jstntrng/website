The Poem Project

How do you build experience? In this project I began to study how installation can be a medium to providing people with an experience. Using a Kinect Sensor, Processing, and projection I took a poem around existential crisis and built an experience that projected the emotion and feeling of the poem onto the viewer. By using appropriate imagery, typography, and pacing, a designed experience can stop someone in their tracks and engage them into a narrative. In this case, the poem’s narrative already had it’s own way of implicating the viewer, the project simply acts as an aid to the poetry.
