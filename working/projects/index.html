SFAC Passport
As a marketing and communications designer at Chronicle Books, I worked on an array of marketing material and helped promote some exciting books from Chronicle's Spring 2017 to Fall 2018 collection.
Working closely with the marketing team I developed print and wevb material through the method of closely examining and pulling from beautifully designed Chronicle books. Final formats include postcards, video end cards, advanced readers book copies, animated web graphics, and display mockups.

For Chronicle's Fall 2018 catalog, I had the opportunity to design the divider page spreads. The colorful and uniquely gridded pages celebrate Chronicle's 25 years of fun and unique gift formats.
